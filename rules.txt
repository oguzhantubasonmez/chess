### 3. LEARNING MODE (MOST IMPORTANT)

- Real-time teaching during gameplay
- Short explanations (max 2 sentences)

Examples:

- "You left your piece undefended."
- "This opening is Queen's Gambit."

- Highlight:
  - Good moves → green
  - Mistakes → red

---

### 4. PRE-MOVE SUGGESTION SYSTEM (CRITICAL)

When the user selects a piece BEFORE moving:

- Analyze all legal moves using Stockfish
- Compare each move to the best move

Visual system:
- Best moves → GREEN squares
- Bad moves → RED squares
- Neutral → no highlight or soft color

Rules:
- Show max 2–3 best moves
- Avoid clutter
- Smooth UI updates

Educational hints:
- On bad move:
  "This move may lose material"

- On good move:
  "This improves your position"
